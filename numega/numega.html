        115% Lowest Price Guarantee
         

        If within two weeks of your order/purchase from LTD Commodities you find the same item advertised for less, we will refund 115% of the difference between our price and our competitor's price.

        An item is considered identical when it has the same manufacturer, model number, features, size and piece count. The item must be in stock and available to purchase. No additional offers in the form of coupons, rebates, promotional offers, free shipping, product bundle pricing or other promotions can be considered in the Price Guarantee. "Price" includes product price only; shipping charges are not applicable.

        To qualify for this exclusive offer:

        • The competitor must have the item in stock and available for purchase with immediate shipping
        • The items must be new and identical (manufacturer, model number, features, size and piece count)
        • Items may not be found or purchased from an on-line auction site, a private individual, overstock liquidation dealer, or from outside the U.S., nor in used condition
        • The advertised price must be provided for verification via untouched print media or reprinted on-line advertisement, and must include the date to ensure pricing is current

        If you find a competitor advertising a qualified purchase for less, simply call 847-295-6058 either before or within two weeks of placing your order, and one of our associates will assist you in taking advantage of our 115% Lowest Price Guarantee. Once we receive the competitor ad you submit for verification, we will refund your original method of payment, or credit a new order for the difference in price PLUS an additional 15% of the difference in our price and the competitor's (not including shipping)!

        Our Price Guarantee is subject to change at any time. We reserve the right not to sell products below our cost. Credit card and account refunds may take up to 72 hours to be applied; refunds issued by check may take up to 10 days.

        This offer is valid on phone, mail, fax and web orders that have been placed.

        Privacy
         

        LTD Commodities LLC holds the privacy and security of our customers in the highest regard. The following data explains what kind of information is gathered on the LTD Commodities website and how the information is used.

        LTD Commodities LLC will treat your email address as confidential information; we will not rent, sell or trade the contents of our email list to any outside parties.

        Does LTD Commodities offer secure online ordering?

        Yes, we do. All customer and order information is securely stored behind a firewall and protected from unauthorized access. Your online session is encrypted using SSL with a certificate provided by Verisign. Therefore, none of your personal information, including credit card number, name and address, can be read as the information travels over the internet. Your credit card number is protected by our encryption methods as soon as we capture it and before it is stored in our database to protect it from potential theft. LTD Commodities does not store your CVV2 credit card security code anywhere on its system.

        What do you do with my email address?

        LTD Commodities LLC collects your email address as part of your registration with us. We use this address to communicate with you about your account. We do not sell your email address to others.

        What kind of information do you collect from me when I visit your web site?

        By interacting with our site, you permit us and our partners to place cookies on your browser, recognize cookies, and identify you for marketing purposes. We do ask for additional information if you would like to place an order, request an order status or a free catalog, or if you need to communicate with us regarding an account issue.

        What are 'cookies' and how do you use them?

        "Cookies" are files that are stored on your device's hard drive by your web browser and are used to improve your online experience. Cookies help us identify account holders and optimize their shopping experience by allowing functions such as online shopping carts. Accepting cookies through your web browser is your personal choice. You can set your browser to disable cookies.

        However, if you choose not to accept cookies, we may not be able to provide you with as good of an experience as one that is specifically tailored to your needs by utilizing the cookie.

        We may also use technologies, such as our own cookies, to provide you with personalized online display advertising tailored to your interests. To disable or manage cookies used for this online advertising, go to your browser's privacy and security settings. We may use the services of third parties to collect and use anonymous information about your visits to and interactions with our website through the use of technologies such as cookies to personalize advertisements for goods and services. We may collect your email address via cookies and pixels on the Website through the use of trusted third party partners. These partners may also combine your email information with other information they have access to such as mailing address so that we may serve relevant marketing offers to you via email and direct mail. To learn more, or to opt-out of receiving advertisements tailored to your interests by our third party partners, visit the Network Advertising Initiative at www.networkadvertising.org/choices.

        Does LTD share information it collects from me with outside parties?

        The information you provided on the website is used to fulfill a product order, a catalog request or research a customer inquiry. The information that you provide is stored in-house separate from the Internet.

        We do occasionally share name and address information with other catalog companies that offer similar products. We will not share any personal financial information. If you are on our catalog mailing list, or order from one of our catalogs, and would prefer not to have your name shared with other catalog companies, please email at MarketingDepartment@ltdcommodities.com and ask that your name not be shared.
